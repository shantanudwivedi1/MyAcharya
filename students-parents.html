For Students & Parents

Understanding how MyAcharya works and the benefits for your family

How MyAcharya Works

Our simple and effective 4-step process

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a free consultation to understand your child's learning needs, goals, and preferences. This helps us match you with the perfect tutor.

2

Tutor Matching

Based on your requirements, we carefully select and introduce you to qualified tutors who specialize in your child's subjects and grade level.

3

Trial Session

Your child attends a trial session to ensure compatibility with the tutor and teaching style. We want everyone to feel comfortable before moving forward.

4

Regular Learning

Once satisfied, we begin regular tutoring sessions with ongoing progress tracking and parent updates to ensure continuous improvement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from students and parents

What grades do you cover?

We provide tutoring for all grades from Class 1 to Class 12, including competitive exam preparation.

How do you ensure tutor quality?

All our tutors are carefully screened, have relevant qualifications, and undergo background verification before joining our team.

Can we change tutors if needed?

Yes, if you're not satisfied with your current tutor, we'll help you find a better match at no additional cost.

What safety measures do you have?

All tutors undergo background checks, and we maintain strict protocols for home visits to ensure student safety.

How flexible is the scheduling?

Very flexible! You can schedule sessions according to your convenience, including evenings and weekends.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, our tutors bring relevant study materials and can recommend additional resources based on your child's needs.
