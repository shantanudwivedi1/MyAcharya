Common Questions

Quick answers to help you get started

How quickly can we start?

We can typically arrange a trial session within 2-3 days of your inquiry, depending on tutor availability in your area.

Is the trial session really free?

Yes, absolutely! The trial session is completely free with no obligations. It's our way of showing you the quality of our teaching.

What happens during the trial?

The tutor will assess your child's current level, understand their learning style, and conduct a sample lesson in the chosen subject.

Can we change tutors later?

Yes, if you're not completely satisfied with your tutor, we'll help you find a better match at no additional cost.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, our tutors bring relevant study materials and can recommend additional resources based on your curriculum.

What are your fees?

Our fees vary based on grade level, subjects, and frequency of sessions. We'll provide detailed pricing during your consultation.
